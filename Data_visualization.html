Bike thefts are most common in Downtown Seattle in August, 2014¶

Figure A shows the regional distribution of bike thefts in three months overlaid with the matched latitude and longitude in the map of Seattle. From figure A, it is obvious that the downtown Seattle is the region with the highest density of data points. Figure B&C shows the number of bike theft in three months at Seattle and Seattle downtown (latitude within 47.58-47.65, longitude within -122.36 - -122.30). These two figures show that bike theft occurred more in July and August both at Seattle and in August at Seattle downtown. To conclude, Bike thefts are most common in Downtown Seattle in August, 2014.

with open('seattle_incidents_summer_2014.csv') as csv_file:
    csv_reader = csv.reader(csv_file, delimiter=',')
    line_count = 0
    bike_thefts=[]
    for row in csv_reader:
        if line_count == 0:
            print(f'Column names are {", ".join(row)}')
            line_count += 1
        else:
            if row[4]=='THEFT-BICYCLE':
                  bike_thefts.append(row)
                  line_count += 1
    print(f'Processed {line_count} lines for bike_theft.')
